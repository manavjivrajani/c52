Interesting facts about camels:Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes. When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Interesting facts about chameleon:Chameleon eyes have a 360-degree arc of vision and can see two directions at once. Chameleons have ballistic tongues that are 1.5-2 times the length of their body. Chameleons can’t hear much.

Interesting facts about pigeon:Pigeons acn understand space and time. They can easily get back to their nest from a distance of 1300 miles. They can be trained to be art snobs and to distinguish written words.

Interesting facts about panda:Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day! Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.
